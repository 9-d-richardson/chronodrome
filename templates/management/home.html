ChronoDrome is a simple website with one purpose: creating and sharing timelines and reading orders. It's meant to be both simple and flexible, which you'll hopefully see when you start making timelines of your own.

What sort of stuff qualifies? Well, pretty much anything that should be read or viewed or consumed in a particular order, whether it's your favorite shows or your favorite series of books.
