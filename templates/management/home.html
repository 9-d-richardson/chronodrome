Welcome to ChronoDrome

  • Make timelines!
  • Track your progress!

ChronoDrome is a website with one purpose: creating and sharing timelines and reading orders. It's meant to be both simple and flexible, like the best of Web 1.0, which you'll hopefully see when you start making timelines of your own.

What sort of stuff qualifies? Well, pretty much anything that goes in a particular order, whether it's your favorite show or your favorite series of books or anything else you feel like.

If you want to create timelines yourself or track your progress through other people's timelines, all you have to do is create an account.

Have feedback, requests, or bug reports? Send them here!
